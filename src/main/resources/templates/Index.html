Are you a flight service provider?

Create a service provider account to manage flight-related records in the system. Providers can add airplanes, create flight schedules, update flight details, and prepare available flights for customer searching and booking.

Airplane Management Add aircraft details such as model, capacity, and airline information.
Flight Scheduling Create flight routes, departure dates, arrival times, and available seats.
Provider Dashboard Access a dedicated dashboard for managing flights and schedules.

Are you airline or booking staff?

Staff members can create an account under an approved flight provider. The selected provider must approve the staff account before the staff member can sign in and access the staff dashboard.

Provider Selection Choose the approved flight provider company you belong to.
Approval Required Your account remains pending until that provider reviews it.
Staff Dashboard Once approved, use the single login page to access staff tools.
